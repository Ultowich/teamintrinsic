we are intrinsic.

We're a Young Enterprise team based at Dr Challoner’s Grammar School in Amersham. As students, we make all the decisions about our enterprise, from deciding on our product/service to creating a business plan, managing our student company finances and selling our product/service.

We aim to provide a useful revision guide and have also addressed the importance of mental well-being during the stressful period leading up to exams. Keeping calm and not being stressed is essential to help performance and achieve success.

our product.

The Study Crate gives you the tools needed to revise effectively. It is aimed at students who are going through exams, allowing them to keep on top of their revision, helping them get more out of their study time and allow them to revise more effectively.

It is designed with the necessities of revision in mind, it contains: pencils, pens, highlighters, ruler, rubber, flashcards, post-it notes, stressball, clear pencil case, our very own Intrinsic revision guide, mental health guide and a blank revison timetable for you to fill in.
